What We Do

Founded in 2002, we are a non-profit residential treatment center helping teen girls (ages 11-18) who are coming out of Sex Trafficking. We keep them safe from the Pimps & Traffickers while restoring them to the women that God created them to be, so they can be Self sufficient & Empowered!!﻿﻿
